Poori Recipe

Description:

Poori is a type of deep-fried bread, made from unleavened whole-wheat flour, originated from the Indian subcontinent. Puris are prepared with wheat flour, either atta (whole wheat flour) or sooji (coarse wheat flour). The dough is either rolled out in a small circle or rolled out and cut out in small circles, then deep fried in ghee or vegetable oil. While deep frying, puris puff up like a round ball because moisture in the dough changes into steam which expands in all directions.

Ingredients:

  • Poori dough
  • oil
  • masala

  2. Eat poori
