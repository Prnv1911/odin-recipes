Dosa Recipe

Description:

A dosa is a thin, savoury crepe in Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice. Dosas are served hot, often with chutney and sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew). Dosas are a common food in South India.

Ingredients:

  • Dosa maavu
  • oil
  • chutney sambar

Steps:

  1. Make dosa
  2. Eat dosa
